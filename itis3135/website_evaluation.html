Website Evaluation

Contrast, Repetition, Alignment, Proximity

WestBound Mag

Westbound Mag, a blogging website is an excellent representation of C.R.A.P webpage. Unlike my websites, it has a simplistic yet clean design that captures the readers' attention. The high quality images combined with vibrant colors provide readers an immersive experience.

    Contrast: The colors are not all over the place and the landing page is easy on the eyes. Westbound uses thier high def pictures as a main focal point, and it definitely captures our attention.
    Repetition: The stacked images setting goes very will with text descritions. The repetitive use of similar fonts, styles, sizes and color binds the design together.
    Alignmnet: All the main contents have been centered and are easy to read. The margin on both sides of the webpage seems to be equal.
    Proximity: The images are stacked neatly, the description which includes dates encases the content of the page.
